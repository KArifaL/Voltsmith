About Synergy World, Inc.


Synergy World, Inc. was founded in 1995 and is based in San Diego, CA, USA. Synergy World processes Gift Cards, Loyalty Cards, and Reward Cards for business owners as well as shopping centers with multi-merchant locations. Their commitment to customer service, and fulfilling the client's needs are unsurpassed in the industry. Synergy World, Inc. has also developed a private-label loyalty card, called the Synergy. With Patent Pending Technology - The Synergy Card is the "ultimate loyalty solution" for businesses who would like to attract & retain customers. Please visit www.synergycard.com for details on the Synergy Card Program.

What We Did for Synergy World, Inc.


VoltSmith developed the embedded software for their special purpose payment - Verifone Omni3750. It involved magstripe card processing and communication techniques. We are still continuing to co-operate Synergy World in the field of reward card processing.

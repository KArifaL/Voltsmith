What Voltsmith Is About

VoltSmith Technology is a system integration company that specializes in industrial control systems. It, however, is also involved in design and implementation of other types of information systems, including banking and telecommunication.

VoltSmith Technology is a registered company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was founded in 2003 as an engineering consulting firm. Currently, its core team is comprised of four full-time engineers. The chief engineer, Yeasir Rahul, is an electrical engineer with long experience in various fields from embedded control systems and smartcards to SCADA-databases and banking industry.

Our team of engineers has been developing cost-effective control systems to improve manufacturing productivity Since 2003.

Our aim is to provide customers with high-quality automated production system that enhance their profitability. We accomplish this by developing new systems or increasing the effectiveness, flexibility and safety of their existing automation applications.

The mission of VoltSmith Technology is to contribute in the industrialization of Bangladesh, and to become the leader in its field.
